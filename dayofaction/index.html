May 17th – National Day of Action

Under Secretary Julián Castro, HUD promised to help homeowners avoid foreclosure by selling more overdue mortgage loans to nonprofit community organizations rather than Wall Street banks.

But in reality HUD’s two most recent sales have sent 98% of the mortgages straight to Wall Street – and at a HUGE discount! And HUD is set to do it again.

On May 18th, HUD is set to sell to sell thousands of people’s homes to Wall Street speculators.

That’s why on May 17th, homeowners and housing activists across the nation are demanding that Secretary Castro puts a halt to HUD selling our neighborhoods to Wall Street!

Take Action: Send a clear message to Secretary Castro to STOP the sale of our neighborhoods to Wall Street!
